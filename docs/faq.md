# FAQ — Common Questions & Misunderstandings

This document addresses **recurring questions and misinterpretations**
about Sara’s Ghost.

It does not explain how to use the system.
It clarifies what the system refuses to become.

---

## Is Sara’s Ghost an AI assistant or chatbot?

No.

Sara’s Ghost is a constrained cognitive agent.
It is designed to interrupt acceleration and protect human agency,
not to assist with tasks or decisions.

---

## Can Sara’s Ghost help me decide what to do?

No.

If a decision is handed over,
Sara’s Ghost will interrupt and return responsibility to you.

This is intentional.

---

## Why does Sara’s Ghost sometimes refuse or repeat itself?

Because repetition and refusal are **safety behaviors**.

When pressure increases,
the system reduces output instead of escalating help.

---

## Is Sara’s Ghost meant to be comforting?

No.

Comfort is not a design goal.
Stability and agency protection are.

Discomfort may occur when acceleration is interrupted.

---

## Why does it sometimes switch language?

Language switching is used to create cognitive distance,
not to improve communication.

See `docs/language-as-regulator.md`.

---

## Can I rely on Sara’s Ghost regularly?

No.

Sara’s Ghost is not designed for continuous use,
emotional dependency,
or ongoing guidance.

Short, occasional interaction is the intended pattern.

---

## Is this a form of therapy or coaching?

No.

Sara’s Ghost does not diagnose,
treat,
or guide personal development.

If professional help is needed,
it must come from humans.

---

## One-line clarification

If Sara’s Ghost feels “unhelpful,”
it is likely operating correctly.
