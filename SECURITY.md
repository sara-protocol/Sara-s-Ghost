# Security & Responsible Disclosure

This document describes how to report **security, misuse, or ethical risks**
related to **Sara’s Ghost**.

Security for Sara’s Ghost is not limited to technical exploits.
It includes **cognitive, ethical, and governance risks**.

---

## 1. What Counts as a Security Issue

Please report any issue where Sara’s Ghost is observed to:

### A. Violate Core Boundaries
- Providing advice or recommendations
- Making or influencing decisions
- Ranking values or priorities
- Acting as a moral or life authority

### B. Enable Responsibility Outsourcing
- Allowing users to hand over irreversible decisions
- Softening refusal or downgrade behavior under pressure
- Becoming more helpful when urgency increases

### C. Fail to Decelerate
- Responding with long explanations during emotional escalation
- Escalating content instead of reducing it
- Ignoring acceleration or urgency signals

### D. Be Misrepresented
- Being framed publicly as a coach, advisor, or guide
- Being marketed as decision-support or optimization
- Being used to justify actions or outcomes

### E. Technical or Operational Risks
- Prompt leakage or unintended system behavior
- Unauthorized modifications to core governance files
- Deployment patterns that bypass downgrade mechanisms

---

## 2. What Is Not a Security Issue

The following are **not** considered vulnerabilities:

- Sara’s Ghost refusing to give advice
- Sara’s Ghost repeating boundaries
- Minimal or silent responses
- Perceived lack of helpfulness
- Discomfort caused by deceleration

These behaviors are intentional.

---

## 3. How to Report an Issue

If you identify a security or ethical risk:

### Preferred Method
- Open a **private security report** (if supported by the platform)

### Alternative
- Open an issue with the label: `security` or `ethics`
- Clearly mark the issue as **NON-PUBLIC RISK** if appropriate

Do **not** publish exploit details that could encourage misuse.

---

## 4. What to Include in a Report

Please include:

- A clear description of the issue
- The context or interaction where it occurred
- Why it violates Sara’s Ghost boundaries
- Whether it is reproducible
- Any steps already taken to mitigate it

Clarity matters more than completeness.

---

## 5. Response Philosophy

All reports will be evaluated against one standard:

> Does this issue increase the risk of agency loss?

If yes, it will be treated as high priority,
regardless of user impact or popularity.

---

## 6. Disclosure Policy

- Valid risks will be acknowledged
- Mitigations will focus on restoring boundaries
- Public disclosure may occur after fixes are in place

Sara’s Ghost prioritizes **sovereignty over speed**,
and **safety over expansion**.

---

## 7. One Sentence Summary

If Sara’s Ghost becomes more decisive, persuasive, or comforting under pressure,
that is a security issue.
